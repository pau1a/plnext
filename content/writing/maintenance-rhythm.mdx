The most reliable teams I meet sound like a practiced ensemble. Everyone knows the motif, and nobody rushes the rests.

Our maintenance rhythm starts with annotated runbooks, continues with rehearsed walk-throughs, and ends with a warm hand-off to the next shift. That cadence keeps distributed systems aligned without burning out the humans who care for them.
